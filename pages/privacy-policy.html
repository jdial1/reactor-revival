Introduction

Welcome to Reactor Revival. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our web application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect and Use

Reactor Revival is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device or, optionally, in your own Google Drive account.

  • Local Storage: Your game progress is saved in your browser's local storage. This data is not transmitted.
  • Google Drive: If you choose to connect your Google account, your game save file will be stored in your Google Drive. The application only has permission to access the files it creates. We do not have access to any other files in your Drive. This feature is provided for your convenience to back up and sync your game data.

Third-Party Services

The optional Google Drive integration is subject to the Google Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your Google account credentials.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
