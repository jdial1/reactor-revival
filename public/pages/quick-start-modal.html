Welcome to Reactor Revival!

Basic Loop:

  • Cells produce Power and Heat .
  • Power is sold for Money .
  • Money buys you better components and upgrades.
  • Eventually, you can prestige to earn Exotic Particles (EP) for powerful, permanent unlocks!
Follow the objectives at the top of the screen to learn the ropes! Click the Power display to sell; click the Heat display to vent.

Getting Started Guide

First Steps:

  • You start with $10. Your first objective is to place a cell. Buy a Uranium Cell ($10) from the Parts panel and place it on the grid.
  • Open the Parts panel (tap the Build Parts â€¦ button or the parts sidebar) to find and select components.
  • Components are organized into Power and Heat tabs.
  • Cells are available in Single, Dual, and Quad configurations, which act like 1, 2, or 4 adjacent cells.

Core Mechanics: Power & Heat

  • Power : Generated by cells. Stored in your reactor's global capacity, which can be increased by building Capacitors.
  • Heat : Also generated by cells. Excess heat goes into the reactor's global capacity, which is increased by Reactor Plating.
  • Meltdown: If your global reactor heat exceeds 200% of its maximum, your entire reactor will melt down, destroying all components!
  • Component Explosion: Individual components also have a heat capacity (containment). If a component's internal heat exceeds this, it will explode.

Heat Management

  • Vents actively remove heat from components they are connected to.
  • Heat Exchangers balance heat between adjacent components, spreading it out.
  • Coolant Cells act as passive heat sinks, absorbing large amounts of heat before being destroyed.
  • Reflectors are crucial! Place them next to Cells to significantly boost their power output.

Automation & Progression

  • Purchase upgrades like Grid Transformer Tuning to automatically sell a percentage of your generated power.
  • Perpetual component upgrades will automatically replace depleted Cells, Reflectors, and Capacitors if you can afford it.
  • Invest in Expand Reactor Rows and Expand Reactor Cols to increase grid size.
  • Selling Parts: Right-click a part to sell it. On mobile, tap a part then choose Sell/Destroy. Refund value decreases as a part takes on heat or is used up.
