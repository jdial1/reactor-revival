[REACTOR INITIALIZATION]

-----------------------------------------
[CORE LOOP]
> CELLS produce POWER and HEAT
> POWER is sold for MONEY
> MONEY buys components and upgrades
> Prestige for EXOTIC PARTICLES (EP)
-----------------------------------------
[QUICK START]
Click POWER display to sell power
Click HEAT display to vent heat
Follow objectives at screen top
-----------------------------------------

[OPERATOR MANUAL]

-----------------------------------------
[FIRST STEPS]
Start with $10 - buy a URANIUM CELL
Open Parts panel to find components
Components split into POWER/HEAT tabs
Cells: Single, Dual, Quad configs
-----------------------------------------
[POWER SYSTEM]
POWER: Generated by cells
CAPACITORS increase storage capacity
Sell power before capacity fills!
-----------------------------------------
[HEAT SYSTEM]
HEAT: Also generated by cells
REACTOR PLATING increases heat limit
200% heat = MELTDOWN (all destroyed!)
Components explode if overheated
-----------------------------------------
[HEAT MANAGEMENT]
VENTS: Remove heat from components
EXCHANGERS: Balance heat between parts
COOLANT CELLS: Passive heat sinks
REFLECTORS: Boost cell power output
-----------------------------------------
[AUTOMATION]
GRID TRANSFORMER: Auto-sell power %
PERPETUAL upgrades: Auto-replace parts
EXPAND ROWS/COLS: Bigger reactor grid
Right-click parts to sell (tap on mobile)
-----------------------------------------
[ENDGAME]
PARTICLE ACCELERATOR: Generate EP
Heat must reach accelerator via exchangers
RESEARCH tab: Spend EP on permanent upgrades
REBOOT: Reset reactor, keep research
-----------------------------------------
